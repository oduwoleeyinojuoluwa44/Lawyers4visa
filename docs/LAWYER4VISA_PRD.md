Background
Yemi Oguntolu and Co is an established UK-based specialist immigration law firm. The
Principal Partner has appeared at every tier of the Immigration and Asylum Chamber and has
previously represented the Secretary of State in immigration proceedings - a distinction held by
very few immigration lawyers in private practice. The firm's practice spans visa applications,
immigration appeals, judicial review, human rights law, sponsor licence compliance, and family
immigration across all categories.
In late 2025, the firm initiated a strategic expansion into the Nigerian market, establishing a
representative office in Lagos Nigeria's commercial capital and the primary UK Visa Application
Centre hub for West Africa. Nigeria is among the top five source countries globally for UK visa
applications, and the firm identified a clear gap: the absence of a properly regulated, UK-based
immigration law firm with an in-market Lagos presence targeting corporate clients and
high-net-worth individuals (HNWIs) seeking reliable, expert guidance on complex UK
immigration matters, a need we are uniquely positioned to fulfil.
The Platform
lawyersforvisas.com is the dedicated digital platform for this expansion. It operates as a
subsidiary brand of Yemi Oguntolu and Co - distinct from the parent firm's website
(yemioguntoluandco.com) and is purpose-built to attract, inform, and convert high-intent visitors
into engaged clients. The site is the firm's primary client acquisition channel in the Nigerian
market and internationally.
Why a Dedicated Website
- Focused SEO strategy targeting UK immigration search terms without diluting the parent
domain's authority
- A brand voice and UX calibrated specifically for Nigerian corporate clients and HNWIs
- A clean, conversion-optimised funnel for consultation bookings, separate from the parent
firm's broader legal services content
- Independent analytics, performance tracking, and content iteration for this market

Market Analysis
Market Overview
Nigeria represents a substantial and growing market for UK immigration services. As Africa's
largest economy with a population exceeding 220 million, Nigeria has strong historical, cultural,
and economic ties with the United Kingdom. The UK remains one of the most sought-after
destinations for Nigerian businesspeople, students, families, and investors.
Key Market Indicators
- Nigeria is consistently among the top five source countries for UK visa applications
globally
- Nigerian nationals represent the largest African diaspora population in the UK
- Growing middle class and HNWI population with disposable income for premium legal
services
- Increasing demand for business and investor visas as Nigerian entrepreneurs seek UK
market access
- Strong demand for student visas to UK universities
Target Market Segmentation
Primary Segment: Corporate Clients
Nigerian businesses requiring UK immigration support for their operations, executives, and
employees. This includes multinational corporations, Nigerian companies expanding to the UK,
and businesses requiring regular UK business travel for their personnel.
Service Requirements:
- Sponsor Licence applications and ongoing compliance
- Skilled Worker and Intra-Company Transfer visas for employees
